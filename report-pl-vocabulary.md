# Polish Report Vocabulary (REPORT_LANG = PL)

> **For the AI:** when `REPORT_LANG = PL`, READ THIS FILE before generating ANY Polish content for the report or for `tmp/section-*.json` files. Copy words from this sheet exactly. Do not reconstruct Polish words from memory - always look them up here first. If a word you need is not listed, take 5 seconds to consciously place every ą/ć/ę/ł/ń/ó/ś/ź/ż in the correct slot before writing.

## Polish alphabet - the 9 diacritics

Lowercase: **ą ć ę ł ń ó ś ź ż**
Uppercase: **Ą Ć Ę Ł Ń Ó Ś Ź Ż**

Never substitute with ASCII. `a` is not `ą`, `s` is not `ś`, `z` is not `ż`.

- **"żądania do serwera"** - requests to the server
- **"wydajność strony"** - page performance
- **"szybkość ładowania"** - loading speed
- **"łącze internetowe"** - internet connection
- **"średni czas odpowiedzi"** - average response time
- **"pamięć podręczna"** - cache (lit. "memory at hand")
- **"zmiany w ciągu ostatnich 30 dni"** - changes in the last 30 days
- **"można wyłączyć"** - can be disabled
- **"należy włączyć"** - should be enabled
- **"zaleca się"** - it is recommended
- **"wymaga uwagi"** - requires attention
- **"w porównaniu do"** - compared to
- **"źródłem problemu"** - the source of the problem
- **"zależnie od"** - depending on
- **"również dotyczy"** - also applies

## Quick self-check before saving

Before saving any Polish section JSON, scan the text and confirm:

1. Every occurrence of "srodow" has "ś" (środow)
2. Every occurrence of "lacze"/"lacz" has "ł" (łącze/łącz)
3. Every occurrence of "zadani" in the sense of "request" has "żą" (żądanie)
4. Every occurrence of "mozn"/"moze" has "ż" (można/może)
5. Every occurrence of "wazn" has "ż" (ważny)
6. Every occurrence of "wiecej" has "ę" (więcej)
7. Every occurrence of "czesc" has "ę" and "ś" (część)
8. Every occurrence of "rowniez" has "ó" and "ż" (również)
9. Every occurrence of "przegladark" has "ą" (przeglądarka)
10. Every occurrence of "dostep" has "ę" (dostęp)

If any of the above is wrong, the section is not ready to save.
